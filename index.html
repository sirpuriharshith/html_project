About Platform

The Rocket Evolution & Launch Technology Explorer is an interactive educational website designed to present the historical development and technological advancements of rockets and space launch systems. The platform explains how rocket technology evolved from early experimental designs to modern reusable launch vehicles used in space exploration and satellite missions. This explorer allows users to study the evolution of rocket technology, launch systems and global missions from NASA, ISRO and SpaceX. The platform also demonstrates algorithm usage such as search and sorting applied to rocket databases.
